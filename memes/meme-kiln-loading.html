Load Your Kiln Like a Pro

5 tips to fit more in — without the disasters
📏Plan in tiers. Group pieces by height before you load. Tall on top, short on bottom.
🛡️Kiln wash everything. Shelves get a fresh coat any time you see a chip. Future-you says thanks.
↔️Half-inch rule. Leave space between glazed pieces. Touching = welded together forever.
🔥Mind the elements. Keep ware 1″ from the walls so heat can move freely.
⚖️Balance the load. Even weight on each post = no shelf collapse mid-fire.
