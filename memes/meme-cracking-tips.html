Stop the Cracks

Why Your Pots Keep Cracking

6 fixes every potter needs to know
1Dry slowly & evenly. Cover loosely with plastic. No fans, no heaters, no rush.
2Keep walls even. Thick bottoms + thin walls = cracks every time.
3Wedge out air. Trapped bubbles expand in the kiln and blow out chunks.
4Compress the bottom. Use a rib after every throw — S-cracks hate this.
5Score & slip joins. Don't just smoosh — push the clay together properly.
6Track your pieces. Patterns emerge when you log what works (and what cracks).
