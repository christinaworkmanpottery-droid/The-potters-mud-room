Quick Tips

Wedging Clay

1Use the heel of your palms — not your fingers. Push forward and down in a rocking motion.
2Ram's head wedging: rotate the clay slightly after each push to create a spiral pattern inside.
3Wedge at least 30 times — or until you can slice through and see zero air pockets.
4Keep your work surface lightly dusted with dry clay — too wet and it sticks, too dry and it drags.
5Consistent pressure matters more than speed. Let your body weight do the work.
