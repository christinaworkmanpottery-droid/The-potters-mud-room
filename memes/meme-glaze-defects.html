Glaze Defects Decoded

5 common disasters & how to fix them
🕳️Pinholes. Slow your bisque & hold the glaze firing at peak temp for 10–15 min.
🕷️Crawling. Dust-free bisque + thinner glaze coat. Wipe greasy fingerprints off.
🪟Crazing. Glaze and clay don't fit. Add silica to the glaze or switch clay bodies.
🫧Blistering. Cone too high or kiln too fast. Cool slowly, fire to the right cone.
🌊Running glaze. Apply thinner near the foot. Always wax the bottom inch.
