Cone Cheat Sheet

Know Your Cones

A potter's quick guide to firing ranges
Cone 06
Low-fire bisque — porous, perfect for bright glazes & underglazes. ~1828°F / 998°C
Cone 04
Earthenware glaze firing — vivid color, softer body. ~1940°F / 1060°C
Cone 6
Mid-range stoneware — the sweet spot: durable & food-safe. ~2232°F / 1222°C
Cone 10
High-fire stoneware & porcelain — vitrified, rich surfaces. ~2345°F / 1285°C
