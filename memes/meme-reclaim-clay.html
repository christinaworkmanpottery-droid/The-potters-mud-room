Save Your Scraps

Recycling Clay Like a Pro

Stop throwing away money — reclaim it
1Dry it out first. Let scraps go bone dry — mixing wet and dry clay makes lumps.
2Slake in a bucket. Cover dry clay with water and walk away. Let it dissolve on its own.
3Use plaster bats. Pour your slip onto plaster and let it absorb the excess water evenly.
4Don't mix clay bodies. Keep stoneware and porcelain separate or you'll get surprises.
5Wedge thoroughly. Reclaimed clay needs extra wedging — air pockets love hiding in there.
6Track your batches. Label what went in so you know what comes out. Future you will be grateful.
