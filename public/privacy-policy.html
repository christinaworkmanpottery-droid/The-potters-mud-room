Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 9, 2025

The Short Version

We collect what we need to run the app. We don't sell your data. We don't track you across the internet. You can delete everything anytime.

This policy explains what information The Potter's Mud Room collects, how we use it, and what control you have over it. We wrote this to be readable by actual humans — not just lawyers.

Who We Are

The Potter's Mud Room is a pottery and ceramics community and tracking app, built by a potter for potters. Our website is www.thepottersmudroom.com.

If you have questions about this policy, reach us at thepottersmudroom@gmail.com.

What We Collect

When you use The Potter's Mud Room, we collect the following information:

  • Account info: Your name, email address, and profile photo
  • Pottery data: Pieces you log (photos, descriptions), clay bodies, glazes, firing logs, and projects
  • Community activity: Forum posts, direct messages between members, events
  • Sales & business: Sales records, contacts/buyers, shopping lists, goals
  • Photos: Images you upload of your work, stored on our servers

What We Don't Collect

  • Location data: We do not collect or track your location
  • Payment card numbers: Payments are processed by Stripe — we never see or store your card details
  • Third-party tracking: No Google Analytics, no Facebook pixels, no ad trackers

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Run the app and provide the features you signed up for
  • Let you track your pottery pieces, materials, firings, and sales
  • Enable community features (forums, messaging, events)
  • Send you emails related to your account (password resets, important updates)
  • Improve the app based on basic, anonymous page view analytics

Analytics

We collect basic page view data to understand how the app is used and where we can improve. This is simple, privacy-respecting analytics — no third-party trackers, no cross-site tracking, no advertising profiles.

Cookies

We use session cookies for authentication only — to keep you logged in while you use the app. We don't use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or any third-party cookies.

Newsletter

Our newsletter is completely optional. You choose whether to sign up, and you can unsubscribe at any time with one click. We won't add you without your permission.

Payments

Payments are handled by Stripe. When you subscribe or make a purchase, your payment information goes directly to Stripe. We receive confirmation of payment but never see or store your card number.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

The only exceptions:

  • Stripe: For payment processing (they have their own privacy policy)
  • Legal requirements: If required by law (court order, subpoena, etc.)

Your Rights & Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app at any time
  • Edit: You can update or correct any information
  • Delete: You can delete your account and all associated data — pieces, photos, messages, everything. Once deleted, it's gone.
  • Export: Contact us if you'd like a copy of your data

To delete your account or request your data, email thepottersmudroom@gmail.com or use the account settings in the app.

Data Storage & Security

Your data (including photos) is stored on our servers. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure authentication.

Age Requirement

The Potter's Mud Room is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn that a child under 13 has created an account, we will delete it and all associated data immediately.

If you believe a child under 13 is using our app, please contact us at thepottersmudroom@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this privacy policy, we'll notify you via email or an in-app notice. Minor wording updates won't trigger a notification, but the effective date at the top will always reflect the latest version.

Contact Us

Questions, concerns, or requests? Reach out:

  • Email: thepottersmudroom@gmail.com
  • Website: www.thepottersmudroom.com
