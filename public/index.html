📖 How to Use The Potter's Mud Room

👋 Welcome, Potter!

The Potter's Mud Room helps you track every piece you make — from wet clay to finished work. No more forgetting which glaze you used or what cone you fired at. Here's how to get the most out of it.

🏺 Tracking Pieces

This is the heart of the app. Each piece gets its own log.

  1. Go to Pieces → click + Add Piece
  2. Give it a name (e.g., "Blue cereal bowl")
  3. Pick your clay body and technique
  4. Add the glazes you used — pick how many coats and the method (dip, brush, spray)
  5. Click Save — done!

Come back later to update the status as it moves through the process. Add photos at each stage so you can see the transformation.

💡 Free accounts: 20 pieces, 1 photo each. Paid plans: unlimited pieces, 3 photos each.

🪨 Clay Bodies

Before logging pieces, add your clay bodies:

  1. Go to Clay → click + Add Clay Body
  2. Enter the name, brand, type, cone range
  3. Add the shrinkage % and cost per bag to track expenses
  4. Note the wet vs fired color — super helpful for planning

🎨 Glazes

Log every glaze in your collection:

  1. Go to Glazes → click + Add Glaze
  2. Choose Commercial (store bought) or Recipe (mix your own)
  3. For commercial: add the brand and SKU so you can reorder
  4. For recipes: add each ingredient and percentage
  5. Add up to 3 photos of the glaze result on different clays

💡 The #1 pain point for potters: "What glaze was that?" This solves it.

🔥 Firing Logs

Track every firing:

  1. Go to Firings → click + Log Firing
  2. Pick the type (bisque, glaze, raku, etc.)
  3. Enter cone, atmosphere, kiln name
  4. Log the speed and whether you used a hold (and for how long)
  5. Link it to a specific piece
  6. After opening the kiln, come back and add your results!

💬 Forum

Connect with other potters:

  • Browse for free — everyone can read posts
  • Post & reply — costs 1 forum token each
  • Add photos & short videos to your posts
  • Buy token packs on the Plans page
  • Top tier members get 10 free tokens/month

Categories include General Chat, Glazes & Combos, Show Your Work, Pottery Casualties 💀 (we've all been there), and more!

💰 Sales Tracking

(Top tier) Log every sale — price, venue, date. See your revenue summary and average price. Export to CSV for tax time.

🛍️ Shop

Check out the Potter's Mud Log — a 38-page printable PDF journal that mirrors the app. Perfect for the studio when you don't want your phone near wet clay. Preview it for free before buying!

⚡ Quick Tips

  • Use the search bar on the Pieces page to find anything fast
  • Filter pieces by status to see what's in progress vs done
  • Add photos at each stage — wet, leather hard, bisque, finished. You'll thank yourself later
  • The Dashboard shows your recent work at a glance
  • Update your Profile to set imperial/metric preferences
