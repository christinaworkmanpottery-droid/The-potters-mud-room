What is The Potter's Mud Room (Potters Mudroom)?

The Potter's Mud Room — also known as "Potters Mudroom" — is a free online pottery tracking app where ceramicists and potters can log their pieces, clay bodies, glazes, firing schedules, and sales all in one place. Think of it as your digital pottery journal and studio organizer.

📖 How to Use The Potter's Mud Room

👋 Welcome, Potter!

The Potter's Mud Room helps you track every piece you make — from wet clay to finished work. No more forgetting which glaze you used or what cone you fired at. Here's how to get the most out of it.

🏺 Tracking Pieces

This is the heart of the app. Each piece gets its own log.

  1. Go to Pieces → click + Add Piece
  2. Give it a name (e.g., "Blue cereal bowl")
  3. Pick your clay body and technique
  4. Add the glazes you used — pick how many coats and the method (dip, brush, spray)
  5. Click Save — done!

Come back later to update the status as it moves through the process. Add photos at each stage so you can see the transformation.

💡 Free accounts: 20 pieces, 1 photo each. Paid plans: unlimited pieces, 3 photos each.

🪨 Clay Bodies

Before logging pieces, add your clay bodies:

  1. Go to Clay → click + Add Clay Body
  2. Enter the name, brand, type, cone range
  3. Add the shrinkage % and cost per bag to track expenses
  4. Note the wet vs fired color — super helpful for planning

🎨 Glazes

Log every glaze in your collection:

  1. Go to Glazes → click + Add Glaze
  2. Choose Commercial (store bought) or Recipe (mix your own)
  3. For commercial: add the brand and SKU so you can reorder
  4. For recipes: add each ingredient and percentage
  5. Add up to 3 photos of the glaze result on different clays

💡 The #1 pain point for potters: "What glaze was that?" This solves it.

🔥 Firing Logs

Track every firing:

  1. Go to Firings → click + Log Firing
  2. Pick the type (bisque, glaze, raku, etc.)
  3. Enter cone, atmosphere, kiln name
  4. Log the speed and whether you used a hold (and for how long)
  5. Link it to a specific piece
  6. After opening the kiln, come back and add your results!

🔒 Security & Privacy

Your data is safe with us. Here's how we protect you:

  • Payments: All credit card info is handled by Stripe — the same payment processor used by Amazon, Shopify, and millions of businesses. Your card number never touches our servers. Ever.
  • Your pottery data is private: Only YOU can see your pieces, clay bodies, glazes, and firing logs. No one else — not even other members — can view your personal logs.
  • Glaze privacy: Your glazes are private by default. They ONLY appear in the Community Glaze Library if you specifically choose to share them. Your secret recipes stay secret.
  • Passwords: Stored encrypted (bcrypt hashed). We can't read your password even if we tried.
  • Profile control: You can make your profile private at any time. You can also block any user.
  • HTTPS: All connections are encrypted in transit.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can other members see my pottery logs?

A: No. Your pieces, clay bodies, glazes, and firing logs are completely private to you. Only you can see them when you log in.

Q: What if I share a glaze in the community — can someone steal my recipe?

A: You choose what to share. If you share a combo or recipe in the Community Glaze Library, other members can see it. If you keep it in your personal glaze library, it's private.

Q: Is my credit card safe?

A: Yes. We use Stripe for all payments. Your card number is never stored on our servers — Stripe handles everything securely.

Q: Can I export my data?

A: Yes! Go to your Profile page and use the export buttons to download your clay bodies, glazes, firing logs, pieces, and sales as CSV files. You can open these in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet app.

Q: What are forum tokens?

A: Forum tokens are used to post and reply in the community forum. 1 token = 1 post or reply. Any monthly membership includes 10 free tokens, and any yearly membership includes 20 free tokens — they roll over as long as your membership is active. You can also buy extra token packs on the Plans page.

Q: Can I cancel my subscription?

A: Yes, anytime. Go to your Profile page and click "Cancel Plan." You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
